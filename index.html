About me

Van-Thach Do received the B.E. degree in electrical and electronics engineering from the Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology, Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam, in 2015 and the Ph.D. degree in mechanical engineering from Kyung Hee University, South Korea, in 2020.
His research interests include robotics, autonomous systems, and robust control for uncertain nonlinear systems.
